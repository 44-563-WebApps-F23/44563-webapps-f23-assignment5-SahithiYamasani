When you decrease the page's width, the second row containing the city names is more likely to break or wrap to the next line first. This is because these city name elements are placed within larger col-md containers. In contrast, the first row, which displays letters, should remain relatively stable on smaller screens. This stability is achieved by using col-sm-2 containers for the letter elements, which allows them to maintain their layout without breaking as easily.

Agra, located in northern India, is famous for the breathtaking Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most iconic symbols of love in the world. It also boasts a rich historical legacy with other notable monuments like the Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri.

More about Agra

Mysore, a city in southern India, is renowned for its opulent Mysore Palace, vibrant festivals like Dasara, and a rich cultural heritage. Known as the "City of Palaces," it offers a blend of history, art, and natural beauty, making it a captivating tourist destination

More about Mysore

Srinagar, in Jammu and Kashmir, India, is renowned for its Dal Lake, houseboats, and Mughal gardens. Despite challenges, it's a serene destination, celebrated for its natural beauty, handicrafts, and warm hospitality, making it a must-visit for a unique cultural experience

More about Srinagar
